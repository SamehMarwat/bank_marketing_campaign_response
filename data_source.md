# Dataset Source

- Dataset: Bank Marketing
- Source: UCI Machine Learning Repository
- URL: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/static/public/222/bank+marketing.zip
- DOI: https://doi.org/10.24432/C5K306
- License: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International where listed by UCI
- Business Use: Marketing campaign response prediction for term deposit subscription.

The original downloaded archive is stored in `data/raw/bank_marketing.zip`, with extracted files under `data/raw/bank_marketing/`.
